## Survey Paper

**Title**: Comprehensive Review of Image-Based Deepfake Detection Techniques and Datasets

**Authors**: Sachin Chaudhary, Praful Hambarde, Akshay Dudhane

**Abstract**: The rapid evolution of generative models has enabled the creation of highly realistic synthetic faces, commonly known as deepfakes. This paper provides a comprehensive survey of image-based deepfake generation and detection techniques from 2018 to 2025.
